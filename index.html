About Project SEARCH

Project SEARCH originated at Cincinnati Children's Hospital Medical Center and was designed to provide immersive vocational training and internship opportunities for young adults with disabilities.

The program focuses on preparing participants for competitive, integrated employment through structured rotations in real workplace environments, supporting long-term career success and workforce readiness.

Success Story: Our Partnership with LEGOLAND

Our partnership with LEGOLAND demonstrates the power and impact of Project SEARCH.

Options For All client Niko Holt was recognized as Model Citizen of the Month for his exceptional contributions. Through the program, he developed daily work plans, demonstrated significant professional growth, and embodied LEGOLAND's core value of "Own Your Craft."

This partnership highlights how Project SEARCH participants make meaningful, measurable contributions in high-performance work environments.
